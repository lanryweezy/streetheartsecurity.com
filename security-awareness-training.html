What is Security Awareness Training?

Security awareness training is a program designed to educate employees about the importance of cybersecurity and how to recognize and prevent cyber threats. It helps to build a culture of security within the organization, ensuring that all employees are aware of their role in protecting sensitive data.

Why is Security Awareness Training Important?

Security awareness training is crucial for protecting an organization from cyber threats. It helps employees to understand the risks associated with cyber attacks, recognize potential threats, and take appropriate actions to prevent data breaches. Effective security awareness training can significantly reduce the risk of cyber incidents.
