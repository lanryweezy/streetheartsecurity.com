Social Engineering Testing

Assessing your organization's susceptibility to human-based manipulation tactics.

What is Social Engineering Testing?

Social engineering testing involves simulating attacks that exploit human psychology rather than technical vulnerabilities. These tests assess how well employees can identify and resist tactics like phishing, pretexting, baiting, and tailgating.

Importance of Social Engineering Testing

Attackers often target the human element as it can be the weakest link in an organization's security. Social engineering tests reveal how vulnerable your employees are to manipulation, providing valuable insights for targeted training and awareness programs.

Our Social Engineering Testing Services

We conduct various types of social engineering tests, including:

  • Phishing Campaigns (Email, SMS, Voice)
  • Pretexting (Creating and using an invented scenario)
  • Baiting (Luring users with a false promise)
  • Physical Security Assessments (e.g., Tailgating, Dumpster Diving)
  • USB Drop Testing
  • Impersonation Attempts

Our Approach

Our tests are carefully planned and executed to simulate realistic attack scenarios without causing actual harm or disruption. We provide detailed reports on success rates, employee responses, and recommendations for improving security awareness and resilience.

Benefits of Our Social Engineering Testing

  • Identify Human Vulnerabilities: Understand how susceptible your employees are to manipulation.
  • Measure Training Effectiveness: Assess the impact of your current security awareness programs.
  • Targeted Training: Develop focused training based on test results.
  • Enhance Incident Detection: Improve employees' ability to recognize and report social engineering attempts.
  • Strengthen Overall Security: Reduce the likelihood of successful human-targeted attacks.
