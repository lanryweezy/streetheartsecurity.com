About Us

Street Heart Security helps in protecting IT infrastructures, edge devices, networks, and data from cybercriminals, by providing offensive cybersecurity services, we help organizations identify weaknesses and vulnerabilities in their systems, we will then follow up with a detailed report that includes recommendations on how to harden the security of the systems.

Our Services

Penetration Testing

Conducting simulated attacks to assess the security of systems and networks, identifying vulnerabilities before malicious actors can exploit them.

Incident Response

Providing expert assistance to manage and mitigate the impact of security breaches, helping organizations recover swiftly and effectively.

Computer Forensics

Analyzing digital evidence to support legal proceedings, uncovering the details of cyber incidents and unauthorized activities.

Expert Witness Services

Offering specialized knowledge and testimony in legal cases involving cybersecurity issues, leveraging extensive industry experience.

Security Awareness Training

Educating employees to recognize and prevent cyber threats, enhancing the human element of organizational security.

Vulnerability Assessment

Evaluating systems to identify weaknesses and provide recommendations for strengthening defenses against potential attacks.

Product Claims Testing

Validating security product claims through rigorous testing, ensuring solutions perform as advertised.

Red Team Operations

Simulating real-world attacks to test an organization's detection and response capabilities, providing insights into defensive readiness.

Social Engineering Testing

Assessing susceptibility to manipulation by attempting to exploit human factors, such as through phishing or impersonation tactics.
