What is Incident Response?

Incident response is the process of preparing for, detecting, responding to, and recovering from security incidents. It involves a structured approach to managing the aftermath of a security breach or cyber attack, ensuring minimal impact on the organization.

Why is Incident Response Important?

Incident response is crucial for minimizing the impact of security breaches. It helps organizations to quickly identify, contain, and mitigate the effects of cyber attacks, reducing downtime and protecting sensitive data. Effective incident response also aids in maintaining regulatory compliance and customer trust.
