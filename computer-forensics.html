What is Computer Forensics?

Computer forensics is the process of investigating and analyzing digital evidence to support legal proceedings. It involves the identification, preservation, collection, and examination of digital information to uncover the details of cyber incidents and unauthorized activities.

Why is Computer Forensics Important?

Computer forensics is crucial for uncovering the truth in legal cases involving digital evidence. It helps organizations to identify the source of cyber attacks, gather evidence for legal proceedings, and ensure compliance with regulatory requirements. Effective computer forensics also aids in maintaining the integrity of digital evidence and protecting sensitive data.
